# Why do I write blogs

When I was in university, I started to write a blog, hoping to make a good impression on the interviewer when I was looking for a job.

I got what I wanted, but also lost the reason to write a blog.

I tried to keep blogging, but failed. Because it took me a long time to write a blog, but nobody read my blog at all.

This situation did not improve untill I found other reasons to write blogs.

Here are the reasons I found for blogging.

## To practice English writing

As a Chinese, my English is very poor. But as a programmer, I need to have a high level of English. So I decided to use blogging to improve my English.

To my surprise, I found that I prefer to write short articles in English, because the articles will have more words and look more beautiful.

I hope to write more essays in the future.

## To self query conveniently

I have a lot of things to look up in my work, but I can't remember so many things, and the search engine may not be able to find answers I need.

After softing these things into blogs, I can use the search engine inside the blog to check my past solutions.

## To monitor the quality of life

There is no doubt that blogging is a time consuming and energy consuming thing. Only when my life is good, can I publish articles stably and efficiently.

As long as I observe that I haven't written a blog for a period of time, I will seriously consider whether there is something wrong with my life.

## Summary

In a word, I write blogs for myself, not for others. That's why I keep writing blogs.
